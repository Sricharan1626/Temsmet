"AI-SIIS 2026" (#68901) – IEEE International Conference on AI and Security for Industrial IoT Systems (AI-SIIS 2026) is a global conference that brings together scientists, engineers, industry leaders, and policymakers to explore the latest innovations in artificial intelligence and industrial electronics related technologies. It is in collaboration with KL University and IEEE SB KLEF. It is technically sponsored by IEEE IES (Industrial Electronics Society). The conference will address key challenges and innovations in securing IIoT systems, leveraging AI to enhance the resilience, autonomy, and safety of industrial environments.

As industries continue to digitize and adopt smart manufacturing, smart cities, and Industry 4.0 technologies, the need for robust AI-driven security solutions has never been more pressing. This conference will showcase cutting-edge research, practical applications, and emerging trends in securing IIoT systems from cyber threats while harnessing the potential of AI to drive industrial innovation.

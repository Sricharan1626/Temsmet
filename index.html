Conference Theme: Innovating for All

Theme: Innovating for All: AI, Sustainability, and the Spirit of Vasudeva Kutumbakam

This conference theme embodies the holistic vision of Vasudeva Kutumbakam—the ancient Indian philosophy that the world is one family—by emphasising inclusive innovation, responsible technology, and global sustainability. In an era shaped by Artificial Intelligence (AI), the theme explores how intelligent systems can be leveraged to create equitable solutions, uplift communities, and accelerate sustainable development across borders.

The conference highlights the transformative role of AI in addressing global challenges, including climate resilience, resource optimisation, healthcare accessibility, innovative governance, and the development of ethical digital ecosystems. It provides a multidisciplinary platform for researchers, scientists, engineers, academicians, industry leaders, and students to exchange cutting-edge ideas, showcase emerging technologies, and present original research contributions.

Featuring keynote sessions, panel discussions, workshops, and invited talks by eminent experts, the conference aims to inspire collaborative innovation grounded in ethics, sustainability, and the shared responsibility of building a better future for all. Original research papers and impactful project contributions are invited from researchers worldwide.

About 5th TEMSMET 2026

The 5th TEMSMET 2026 continues the prestigious global conference series of the Technology and Engineering Management Society (TEMS), serving as the annual flagship platform for thought leadership in technology, innovation, and engineering management. Since its inception in 2020, the TEMSMET series has consistently brought together researchers, practitioners, and professionals from around the world to explore the evolving intersections of technological advancement and strategic management practices.

This 5th edition embraces the transformative theme “Innovating for All: AI, Sustainability, and the Spirit of Vasudeva Kutumbakam”, reflecting a vision of inclusive innovation, responsible technological growth, and global unity. This edition of the conference will provide a dynamic forum to deliberate on how Artificial Intelligence, sustainable development, and humanity-centric values are shaping the future of technology management.

The conference invites original research, case studies, and innovative solutions that present interdisciplinary approaches, novel frameworks, implementation models, ethical considerations, and global best practices. Contributions that demonstrate how innovation and AI can drive sustainable and equitable progress across industries, societies, and nations are especially encouraged.

Attendees will benefit from a rich program featuring:

  • Plenary sessions by distinguished global experts
  • Invited talks and panel discussions spanning technology, management, sustainability, and societal impact
  • Presentation of high-quality research papers, selected through a rigorous peer-review process
  • Networking opportunities for academicians, researchers, industry leaders, students, entrepreneurs, policy thinkers, and practitioners

The 5th TEMSMET 2025 aims to foster collaborative learning, inspire impactful research, and promote a shared commitment to advancing technology for the benefit of all—truly echoing the spirit of Vasudeva Kutumbakam.

About the Host: SIT Hyderabad

Symbiosis Institute of Technology (SIT) Hyderabad, established in 2024, is one of the newest constituent institutes of Symbiosis International University (SIU), Pune—a recognised leader in India's private university sector. Inspired by the vision of Chancellor Dr S.B. Mujumdar and founded on the guiding philosophy of "Vasudhaiva Kutumbakam" (the world is one family), SIT Hyderabad is committed to delivering world-class technical education in a vibrant and inclusive academic environment.

Located in Hyderabad, a city ranked among the best in India for livability, innovation, education, and technology-driven growth, the institute is rapidly emerging as a hub for high-quality engineering education. SIT Hyderabad offers contemporary B.Tech programmes in Computer Science and Engineering, Computer Science and Technology, Computer Engineering, and Artificial Intelligence & Machine Learning.

Symbiosis International University (SIU) is a prestigious, multidisciplinary university recognised for its global outlook, academic excellence, and commitment to inclusive education. The university has earned strong national and international recognition.

Conference Structure

Keynote Talks

Delivered by globally recognised experts in Artificial Intelligence, Engineering, Sustainability, and Technology Management, offering deep insights aligned with the conference theme.

Panel Discussions

Engaging dialogues addressing contemporary issues, including AI ethics and governance, sustainable technological innovation, the future of engineering and management education, and the societal impact of AI-driven solutions.

Technical Sessions

Tracks featuring high-quality research papers and case studies focusing on emerging technologies, interdisciplinary innovations, AI applications, sustainability frameworks, and advances in engineering and management practices.

Workshops

Hands-on, skill-building sessions that enable participants to explore AI tools, sustainable engineering methods, data-driven decision-making techniques, and innovation management frameworks through practical exercises.

Poster Sessions

A platform for researchers, innovators, and students to present preliminary findings, concept demonstrations, and ongoing work aligned with the conference theme and its multidisciplinary scope.

Networking Events

Curated networking opportunities, meetups, and informal interactions designed to foster collaboration between academia, industry, startups, policymakers, and research organisations.

Potential Speakers

Industry Leaders

CEOs, CTOs, Innovation Officers, and senior technology executives from leading global and national organisations, contributing insights on cutting-edge AI applications, digital transformation, and sustainable technological growth.

Academicians & Researchers

Professors, scientists, and scholars from premier universities and research institutions specialising in Artificial Intelligence, engineering, management, sustainability, and interdisciplinary innovation.

Government Representatives

Policymakers, administrators, and officials involved in national AI strategies, technology governance, regulations, and initiatives supporting ethical, secure, and sustainable technological development.

Entrepreneurs

Startup founders, innovators, and emerging technology leaders developing next-generation AI-driven products, solutions, and platforms across engineering, sustainability, and management domains.

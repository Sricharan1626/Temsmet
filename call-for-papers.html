Conference Scope

TEMSMET 2026 focuses on industrial and upcoming frontier technologies, applications of electronics, controls, communications, instrumentation and computational intelligence. The objectives of the conference are to provide high quality research and professional interactions for the advancement of science, technology, and fellowship. Papers with new research results are encouraged for submission.

The conference is technically sponsored by IEEE IES (Industrial Electronics Society) and utilizes the Microsoft CMT service for peer-reviewing.

Technical Topics

Track 1: Sustainable Power, Energy Systems & Green Technologies

  • Sustainable Power Generation & Transportation Systems
  • Renewable and Clean Energy (Solar, Wind, Green Hydrogen, Power-to-X)
  • Power Electronic Converters, Topologies & Control
  • Digital Twins for Power Electronic and Energy Systems
  • Electric & Hybrid Electric Vehicles, Charging Infrastructure
  • AI and Data Analytics in Power & Energy Engineering
  • IoT Integration in Smart Energy Systems
  • Smart Grids, Resilience & Energy Commerce
  • Cybersecurity for Cyber-Physical Energy Systems
  • Sustainable Energy Policy, Carbon Neutrality & Global Impact

Track 2: Artificial Intelligence, Data Science & Cybersecurity Governance

  • Recurrent Neural Networks (RNN), LSTM & Deep Learning Architectures
  • Generative AI, Foundation Models & Multimodal Systems
  • Transformers and Large Language Models (LLMs)
  • Image/Video Processing, Object Detection & Recognition
  • AR/VR for Engineering, Education & Human-Centric Solutions
  • Predictive Analytics & Forecasting in Real-World Scenarios
  • Big Data Analytics & Applied AI in Healthcare & Finance
  • AI Ethics, Bias, Transparency, Safety & Responsible AI
  • Cybersecurity, Data Privacy, Digital Trust & Zero Trust Architecture
  • AI Laws, Policies & Global Governance Frameworks
  • Risk Modelling, Threat Detection & Security Challenges in the AI Era

Track 3: Smart Systems, Communication Technologies & Intelligent Infrastructure

  • IoT, IIoT, Sensor Networks & Edge/Fog Computing
  • Smart Cities, Smart Homes & Human-Centred Smart Services
  • Blockchain, Cryptography & Privacy-Preserving Technologies
  • Wireless Networks, Optical Communications & Sensor Networks
  • RF, Microwave & mmWave Innovations
  • Embedded Systems, Real-Time Systems & Cyber-Physical Devices
  • Software-Defined Networking (SDN) & Network Function Virtualisation (NFV)
  • High-Performance Computing for Engineering Applications
  • Evolution to 5G, 6G & Future Connectivity
  • Resilient Digital Infrastructure for Society

Track 4: Engineering, Digital Management & Human-Centred Technology

  • Software Engineering & Smart Management Practices
  • Smart Manufacturing, Automation & Industry 5.0
  • Predictive Maintenance & AI-Driven Production Systems
  • Digital Twins for Business, Supply Chain & Operations
  • Organisational Change & Workforce Transformation in the Digital Era
  • AI-Enhanced CRM, Service Delivery & Personalisation
  • CFO Tech: AI-Powered Financial Forecasting & Risk Analytics
  • AI-Augmented Decision Making & Strategic Management
  • Digital Currencies, CBDCs & Policy Considerations
  • Cloud-Based Analytics & Agile Business Platforms
  • Human-Tech Collaboration in Future Workplaces

Track 5: VLSI, Robotics, Embedded Systems & Intelligent Signal Processing

  • Audio, Acoustic, Image, Video & Multidimensional Signal Processing
  • Computer Vision & Intelligent Perception Systems
  • Mobile Robotics, Outdoor Service Robots & Autonomous Navigation
  • Aerial Robots: GNC, Autonomy & AI Integration
  • Human–Machine Interaction & Multirobot Coordination
  • AI-Assisted VLSI Design, EDA Frameworks & Nanoelectronic Systems
  • Neuromorphic Computing Trends & Brain-Inspired Systems
  • Quantum Computing for Engineering & Optimisation
  • Deep Submicron Design Challenges & Testing Methodologies

Track 6: Innovation, Entrepreneurship & Inclusive Digital Economies

  • Disruptive Product Innovations & Scalable Ventures
  • Inclusive and Sustainable Business Models for Social Impact
  • AI-Powered Sales, Marketing & Consumer Intelligence
  • Startup Acceleration, Funding Strategies & Market Dynamics
  • Technology Commercialisation & Innovation Management
  • Frugal Innovation, Grassroots Innovation & Community Tech
  • Women in Tech Leadership & Youth Entrepreneurship
  • Global Collaboration Models under Vasudhaiva Kutumbakam

Track 7: Law, Ethics, Public Policy & Human-Centred Governance

  • AI Regulations, Privacy Laws & Global Digital Governance
  • Legal Challenges around Autonomous Systems & Robotics
  • Ethical Dilemmas in AI, Data Use & Automation
  • Policy Frameworks for Sustainable Technology Adoption
  • Tech Diplomacy, Cross-Border Data Flow & Digital Sovereignty
  • Environmental Policies & Sustainable Urban Planning
  • Governance for Smart Cities, Mobility & Green Energy
  • Human Rights, Digital Inclusion & Equity

Track 8: Education, Society & the Future of Human–AI Coexistence

  • AI in Education, Personalised Learning & Smart Classrooms
  • Future Skills, Workforce Development & Lifelong Learning
  • Role of Humanities & Social Sciences in the AI Era

  • Human–AI Collaboration & Responsible Coexistence

  • Technology for Accessibility & Assistive Solutions

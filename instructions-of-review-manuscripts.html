Instructions for Reviewing Manuscripts

Core Reviewer Guidelines

Please ensure the manuscript adheres to the official formatting standards for the TEMSMET 2026 Research Track.

View Official IEEE Paper Templates

1. LENGTH AND COLUMN FORMAT

Length: Manuscript must be strictly between 2 and 5 pages (inclusive of figures, tables, and references).

Format: Must use the 2-column layout. Papers outside these limits must be rejected.

2. TEXT AND APPEARANCE

Language: The entire paper must be in English.

Font: Use Times-Roman or Computer-Modern fonts. Size should be between 10 and 12 points.

Title & Authors: The TITLE should be in **BOLDFACE ALL CAPITALS**. Author lists must exactly match the online submission form.

3. STRUCTURE AND CITATION

Headings: Major headings must be BOLD CAPITAL and centered. Subheadings should be underlined or boldface.

References: All references must be numbered and cited in square brackets (e.g., [1]).

Illustrations: Must be clear, captioned, numbered, and positioned within designated margins. They should be legible if printed in black-only.

4. INTEGRITY POLICIES (MANDATORY)

Reviewers must confirm the paper is **original**.

The paper must **NOT contain plagiarized material**.

The paper must **NOT be submitted to any other conference** at the same time (double submission).

PAGE NUMBER RESTRICTION

Reviewers: DO NOT INCLUDE PAGE NUMBERS on the document being reviewed. Appropriate page numbers will be added during final conference proceedings assembly.

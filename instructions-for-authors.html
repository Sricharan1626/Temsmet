Instructions for Authors

Paper Submission Guidelines

Format and Length Requirements:

  • Papers must be submitted in PDF format.
  • Maximum length is 5 pages (double-column format), including all figures, tables, and references.
  • You must use the standard IEEE template: [Official IEEE Templates Link]
  • Papers not compliant with the template or exceeding the page limit will be returned without review.

Commitment and Publication

  • Only original papers that have not been published or submitted elsewhere will be considered.
  • Submission of a paper implies a commitment that, upon acceptance, the authors will submit their camera-ready version and present the paper at the conference.
  • Registration Requirement: Each accepted contribution must have at least one paid registration by the camera-ready deadline.
  • Publication: All Accepted and Presented papers will be submitted for publication to IEEE Xplore, which is indexed with leading Abstracting & Indexing (A&I) databases (e.g., SCOPUS).

Submission Deadline: 15 February 2026

Submit Your Paper via CMT

Microsoft CMT service is used for managing the peer-review process.

Review Process and Integrity

Review Details

  • All papers undergo a rigorous, single-blind reviewing process.
  • Accepted papers will be assigned to a regular technical session.
  • E-Copyright Form: Must be submitted by the author or presenter at the time of registration.
  • Papers must satisfy the requirements given in the publication policy.

IEEE Policies on Integrity

  • Plagiarism / Double Submission: Papers are reviewed on the basis that they do not contain plagiarized material and have not been submitted to any other conference at the same time (double submission). These matters are taken very seriously.
  • No-Show Policy: IEEE reserves the right to exclude a paper from distribution (e.g., by not including it in IEEE Xplore) if the paper is not presented at the conference.
